# Shortcuts

This guide uses following scheme to describe keyboard "shortcuts":

* `+` — simultaneous pressing;
* `,` — sequential pressing.

To enter special symbols specified in this section, use the following:

* In **macOS**, the option key works as a modifier, as well as as the shift key. Press the key and press the required key simultaneously. To combine the option + shift keys, press the option key and the shift key simultaneously.
* In **Windows**, the alt key works as a modifier, as well as as the shift key. To enter a special symbol, press the alt key and enter the numbers on the number block of the keyboard (right). The NumLock indicator should be on.
